Common Questions

Quick answers to questions we hear often.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! Most of our clients are remote. We use modern collaboration tools and have a proven process for keeping projects on track regardless of location.

What's your typical project timeline?

It varies by project scope. Small integrations or MVPs might be 4-8 weeks. Larger applications can be 3-6 months. We'll give you a clear timeline in our proposal.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Absolutely. We offer monthly retainers for ongoing support, bug fixes, enhancements, and technical guidance. Many clients start with a project and continue with a retainer.

What if I'm not sure exactly what I need?

That's totally fine! Many clients come to us with a problem but aren't sure of the solution. We'll help you figure out the best approach during our initial consultation.
